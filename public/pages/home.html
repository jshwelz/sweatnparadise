The Sweat and Paradise Experience

An Island in the middle of the Caribbean will become your own gym, get to know the soul of this place and work out in the process by being part of the Sweat and Paradise training retreat.The experience involves outdoor training in various spots throughout the island, running through nature surrounded by the sound of plants and wind, nights out getting to know the rythm of the island, Sunset yoga in a pavillion 90ft (30m) above the crushing waves against rocky cliff and pushing your body to its limits in a complete functional training center. Check out the List of Activities that our different programs include.

All these activities will take a toll on you and your body will need to recover, to comply, you will stay in one of our luxury Villas located throughout the island with everything you need to get back your stamina. Furthermore, be assesed by our nutrition specialist before arriving to camp in order to fit your caloric intake requirements to last through this camp. Get the best of you by retreating and connecting with your body. Live the Sweat N' Paradise experience.

Luxury Villas for Accomodation

Stay in one of the Luxury villas we provide for the accomodation of the team that will spend the week with us. The guests of the house will be received by the house host and make sure you rest and be feed accordingly.

Discover Roatan like a local

Throughout the program we will visit different spots in the island, spots that are not meant to be "touristic" in an effort to show you the real Roatan, beautiful hidden gems across the island including hidden beaches and desolated buildings.

Detailed diet Control

Receive all the nutrients through amazing dishes designed by our chef Christina Morataya in hand with our nutritionist Katia A. Motta to make sure the nutrients in your food are correct and you are ready to go when it comes to training.
